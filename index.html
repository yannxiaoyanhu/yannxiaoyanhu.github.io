About me

As a Year 3 PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), I’m blessed to be under co-supervision of Professor Ho-fung LEUNG and Professor Farzan FARNIA.

Prior to that, I received my bachelor degree in Statistics from the School of mathematics, Sun Yat-sen University.
